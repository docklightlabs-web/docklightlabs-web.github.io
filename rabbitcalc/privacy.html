RabbitCalc — Privacy Policy

Overview

RabbitCalc is developed by Docklight Labs. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

RabbitCalc collects no personal information. No account is required. No data is sent to any server. All calculations happen locally on your device.
