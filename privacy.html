Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Docklight Labs builds simple, offline-first Android apps. This policy applies to all apps published under the Docklight Labs developer account on Google Play.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Our apps do not require accounts, logins, or registration of any kind.

Local Storage

Some apps store data locally on your device — such as lists, settings, or preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Internet Access

Most Docklight Labs apps do not require internet access and function entirely offline. Any app that does access the internet will disclose this clearly in its Play Store listing.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services in our apps. We do not sell, share, or transmit any user data to third parties.

In-App Purchases

Some apps offer optional one-time in-app purchases via Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Permissions

Any device permissions requested by our apps (such as file access) are used solely to provide app functionality and are not used to collect personal information.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
